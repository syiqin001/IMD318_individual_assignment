About Me

Hi, Assalammualaikum! My name is Nurul Asyiqin binti Amrillah, and I’m proudly from Rantau Panjang, Kelantan 🌸 I’m currently pursuing my Diploma in Information Management at UiTM Machang. Ever since I began this journey, I’ve grown deeply passionate about managing information, organizing records, and turning messy data into something meaningful and useful.

I enjoy building simple yet functional websites, designing digital posters, and exploring new tools, especially Canva and coding with HTML & CSS. Creating things that are both beautiful and informative truly brings me joy! 🎀

Aside from academics, I’m a detail-oriented person who believes in the power of consistency and creativity. I love taking part in real-world projects, particularly those involving digital systems, archive management, and records processing. I find fulfillment in helping others stay organized and informed.

When I’m not studying or coding, you can probably find me journalling, painting with soft pastel tones, hunting cafés with pretty desserts, or capturing little aesthetic moments on my camera 📷🧁🎨 These hobbies help me express myself and bring calmness to my world.

I always strive to improve, learn new things, and make people smile with what I do. My goal is to become someone useful, not only in my field but also in the lives of others. I believe that even small details can make a big impact. ✨
